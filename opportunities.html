Our Top Picks

Here are a handful of our favourite volunteering opportunities, both globally or in the UK:

  • Forestry England allow people to volunteer in forest management to make sure that the nations forests thrive
  • Refuge Action help people who are environmental refugees as a result of climate change.
  • Nature Volunteering helps volunteers to find UK conservation projects that match their interests in helping nature.
  • Environmental Agency Flood Wardens The Environment Agency seeks volunteers to play a vital role in protecting communities from flooding.
  • WWF is creating an environmental conservation movement in India that aims to inspire people to care for nature. Their volunteers program addresses this by building a community of conscious and empowered citizens.
  • Climate Ed offer free climate workshops in schools, teaching children about climate science, carbon literacy and climate action.
  • British Red Cross involves helping out in emergency situations such as natural disasters, fires, floods, individual medical emergencies and transport accidents. As an emergency response volunteer you'll support the emergency services, provide first aid and transport assistance, offer practical and emotional support to people in crisis and help to organise evacuations.
