Common FAQ's

Why volunteer abroad? Our volunteering abroad program offers an immersive experience like no other. Engage with diverse cultures, learn new languages, and tackle pressing global issues. Be part of transformative projects, from empowering underprivileged communities to conserving precious environments. Join us to create lasting memories, forge international friendships, and leave a positive mark on the world.
What type of volunteer work can I do? We are proud to offer a range of affordable volunteer projects both abroad or locally. Whether you want to teach, support conservation projects, volunteer with animals, or provide support within childcare centers, we have volunteer opportunities to suit all interests and skill sets.
Can people under the age of 18 volunteer? Yes, most programs accept under 18 volunteers when traveling with a parent or guardian. Volunteers under the age of 18 will be required to provide HandInHand with parental or guardian consent plus two character reference letters in order to participate, and may be asked to provide further documentation to the local team depending on their chosen project.
What are the requirements to volunteer with HandInHand? Volunteer requirements vary from project to project, however for the majority, the only requirements are a high level of competence in English, a clean criminal background, and a willingness to learn. You do not need to have volunteered before and you do not need to have experience in the type of volunteering you wish to undertake.
